Compressor 1.5.0 adds new features:
- EXIF data is now retained when compressing videos
- Brand new, cleaner, tabbed UI for phones
- Brand new, cleaner, 2-pane UI for tablets
- More responsive overall UI, with haptics, animations and smoothing
- Ability to adjust volume of the final video
- Inapplicable presets are now greyed out rather than hidden
- Added a new error screen to clearly detail errors when they occur
- 'Report Bug' button
- Compressed videos larger than original now count as failed, with an option to save anyways
- Logs can now easily be copied to clipboard from the 'Report Bug' modal

Bug Fixes:
- Fixed Original Audio Bitrate chip consistency with other chips
- Orientation is now correctly locked to portrait on phones
