Compressor 1.4.0 adds new features:
- Audio compression and bitrate selector (solves https://github.com/JoshAtticus/Compressor/issues/32)
- Japanese translation
- Improved user interface, "Original" chips now display original value, final video resolution and frame rate now displayed underneath estimated size, Less Space/Balanced/Higher Quality markers on target size slider, target size now displayed above slider

Bug Fixes:
- "Show Bitrate" toggle is now persistent across restarts (solves https://github.com/JoshAtticus/Compressor/issues/34)
